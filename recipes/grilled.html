Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

This simple but tasty snack is a pivotal food for anyone looking for a quick bite.

Ingredients

  • PepperJack or assorted Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Put butter on side of pan.
  2. After butter melts, put bread on to simmer.
  3. Put pepperjack or equal cheese on but sides of bread.
  4. Allow the cheese to melt and the bread to turn a golden brown.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
