Kale Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

A green salad of romaine with a highly seasoned dressing of pounded anchovies, olive oil, lemon juice, egg, and Parmesan cheese, garnished with croutons.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups chopped fresh kale
  • 4 Cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 2 Cups croutauns
  • 3/4 Cups parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add ingredients together and mix.
  2. Serve immediately.
